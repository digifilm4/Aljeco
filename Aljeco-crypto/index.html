Welcome to The Allen Jean Company

Allen Jean Company began under HIE in Wellington, New Zealand in 2009. Joshua was an Artist in Residence (AIR) working on a painting series “Cubist Impression: Houghton Bay”, preparing a gallery exhibition at A’Art Gallery in Newtown, New Zealand and quickly going broke doing it. Joshua’s painter jeans had been wore out in the process and remembering back to close friend and fellow painter, Christina Diaz’s, suggestion, “Who cares, just wear your painting jeans as a style.” As the jeans were tattered and ripping, Joshua patronized a local ‘give a pair/take a pair’ second-hand clothing store and began sewing back together his tattered jeans with other wore out jeans. Word-of-Mouth spread and people started asking where they could buy a pair and at first Joshua responded with how he made them. People were not interested in making the jeans, just buying them and thus Allen Jean Company was formed.

Today, Allen Jean Company offers non-solicitation jeans, art-inspired apparel, footwear and fine art prints & accessories.

The Work

"It is your work in life that is the ulimate seduction" - Pablo Picasso

Joshua,owner of Allen Jean Company, is a professional artist working in the visual arts, filmmaking and writing. He graduated with a B.A. from the University of Kentucky majoring in English Literature and Film Studies and attended the California Institute of the Arts M.F.A. Film Directing Program. After a lengthy hiatus he has returned to the United States and is currently working on his fourth series of painting studies he calls ‘Cubist Impressionism’. Along his travels Joshua was urged to monetize and merchandise his custom made attire and works of art.

Today, Joshua resides in his home city of Louisville, Kentucky and continues to work in his private studio. Partnerships stretch from Australia/New Zealand to Europe to Taiwan.
